Common questions about our photography services

We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance for weddings, especially during peak season (October to March). For other events, 2-3 months in advance is usually sufficient.

Yes, we frequently travel across India for weddings. Travel expenses are calculated separately based on the location. We've covered weddings in Delhi, Goa, Rajasthan, Kerala, and many other destinations.

For weddings, we typically send a team of 2-3 photographers to ensure comprehensive coverage of all ceremonies and moments. For smaller events, a single photographer may be sufficient.

Our standard wedding packages include pre-wedding consultation, full-day coverage by professional photographers, professionally edited high-resolution images, online gallery, and a premium wedding album. Additional services like videography, drone coverage, and special albums can be added.
